About me

My name is Bram van Elderen and I am a software engineer by 
profession and also a game developer by hobby

I have always had a big interest in anything related to computers from a young age, mainly due to me also being an avid gamer early on. Due to this i started programming from a young age, starting on writing some simple programs on a TI-84 calculator to assist me during my school days.

Later i followed the "Informatica" (or Computer Science in english) course at the Hague University, at which point I could actually write some proper code and build cool software. 

But because i always had an passion for game development before finishing the study i took a gap year to develop my own games, on my own but also gathering other like minded people and working together on games in a team.

After the gap year i finished the study at the Hague University by doing an intership for Fenêtre, that is where i stayed working as developer ever since.
