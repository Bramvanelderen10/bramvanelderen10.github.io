Rancharoo

Description

You’re the best rancher in the Galaxy. You know it, the council knows it, everyone knows it.

You don’t need silly looped ropes, zapping weapons, or barking furry creatures. No, you use the land itself. That is how a real rancher herds.

Other Information

Rancharoo is game made in under 72 hours for the Ludum Dare 40 game jam. We've made the game with a team of 3. The main challenge for the game was getting the AI right, since the AI basicly is the whole gameplay. In the end i'm very happy with how technicly complex the ai is and how great it works.

Rancharoo has been released on Itch.Io
